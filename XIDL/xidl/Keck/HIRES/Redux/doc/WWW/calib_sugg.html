Suggested Calibrations for HIRES Observations

 The following are simply recommendations and do not insure optimal data.
  • 10 'Standard' flat images (i.e. with the science setup)
    • Aim for a peak of 30000DN on the chips and a minimum of 1000DN for each order (may be challengin in the blue).
    • It may be important to take a set of Arcs right before and/or after .
  • Arcs
    • Take one pair with the trace flats. For essentially all setups, 1s with the NG3 filter is fine.
    • Take one before each science exposure. This may not be necessary and if you are worried about persistance you may wish to avoid this.
  • Recommended but not required: One spectrophometric standard star (per setup)
